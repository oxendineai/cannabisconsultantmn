What We Do

Full-Spectrum
Cannabis Consulting

Every engagement is handled personally by Alexander Oxendine — not a junior associate. You get direct access to the expertise that built Minnesota's most successful tribal cannabis operation.

01
License Strategy & Application
Full OCM license application management for all license types — microbusiness, cultivator, retailer, mezzobusiness, and more. We know what the state wants and how to give it to them.
02
Final Plans of Record & SOPs
Comprehensive FPOR packages and Standard Operating Procedures built to OCM standards. The documentation that takes you from pre-approved to fully licensed and operational.
03
Facility Assessment & Buildout
On-site evaluation, infrastructure planning, cultivation layout, HVAC, lighting, security design, and full buildout consulting. We've built facilities from the ground up — we know exactly what works.
04
Business Planning & Financials
Investor-ready business plans, financial modeling, and operational projections tailored for Minnesota's market. Built for licensing boards, investors, and lenders alike.
05
Municipal & City Council Strategy
Local approval navigation, zoning strategy, and city council presentation development. After January 2025, Minnesota cities cannot block licensed cannabis businesses — we help you move forward confidently.
06
OCM Compliance & Ongoing Support
Regulatory monitoring, compliance audits, deficiency notice response, and ongoing OCM navigation. The cannabis landscape in Minnesota moves fast — we keep you ahead of it.
